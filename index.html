Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Our School

What age groups does Orchid School serve?

We serve students from Playgroup (PG) starting at age 3, through Class 10. We also offer +2 (Higher Secondary) as an optional program for students who wish to continue their education with us.

What is the medium of instruction?

English is the primary medium of instruction, but we also teach Nepali language and literature as part of our curriculum to maintain cultural connection.

What are the school timings?

School hours are from 9:00 AM to 3:30 PM for regular classes. Early years (PG to Class 2) have slightly shorter hours. Office hours are 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM on weekdays and 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM on Saturdays.

Do you provide transportation services?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable school bus transportation covering major areas in and around Sarlahi. Our buses are equipped with safety features and are driven by experienced drivers.

What facilities does the school offer?

We offer modern classrooms, well-equipped science laboratories, a comprehensive library, computer lab with internet access, sports facilities, and transportation services. Our campus is designed to provide a conducive learning environment.

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply for admission by visiting our school office, calling us at 046-530654 or 9765733679, or emailing us at admissions@orchidschool.edu.np. We'll guide you through the application process and required documents.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer various extracurricular activities including sports, arts and crafts, music, dance, debate, science clubs, and cultural programs. We also participate in inter-school competitions and events.

How do you ensure student safety?

Student safety is our top priority. We have CCTV surveillance, secure campus access, trained staff, regular safety drills, and strict protocols for visitors. Our transportation service also follows strict safety guidelines.
